The Benefits of Composting at Home

  • Waste reduction by diverting organic material from landfills
  • Enriching the soil with essential nutrients
  • Carbon sequestration: compost stores carbon from organic materials and contributes to reduction of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere
  • The reduction of methane emissions; when organic materials are thrown into landfills, they produce methane, a potent greenhouse gas

    Why Food Waste Going to the Landfill is Dangerous

    When compostable waste goes to a landfill, it gets buried under massive amounts of other trash, cutting off a regular supply of oxygen for the decomposers thus slowing down all the compost in the landfill
